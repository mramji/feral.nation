Welcome to Feral Nation

What We Do

We're a non-profit cat rescue with our HQ situated in Roseneath, Wellington.

Our Mission

To protect cats and kittens that have been abused, abandoned or forgotten. We provide a safe haven for community/stray cats and kittens and aim to spay/neuter, socialise, vaccinate, microchip and find homes for as many of them as possible.
