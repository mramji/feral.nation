Interested in Fostering?

"Fostering is good for the soul." - Amelia & Jon, FN Fosterers

Open up your home and your heart to those who were overlooked. Fostering is a very rewarding experience, and costs you nothing - we provide all supplies, cover vet bills, and provide any additional support and advice you might need. Your job is to provide love and edicate your time to thesse vulnerable creatures. The goal is to help them lto leave in a better state than they came to you in.

In order to foster for Feral Nation you must be located near our HQ - so that's in Roseneath, Hataitai, Kilbirnie, Lyall Bay, Miramar or Newtown - in case of illness. Exceptions to this are if you're a vet or vet nurse; in these instances you just need your own transport.

We predominantly use our Facebook page as our point of contact.
