Adopt

"The cat chooses its family."

At Feral Nation, like the wands in Harry Potter the kittens choose who they want to live with. Many of our cats and kittens have been rescued from poor living situations, and had so much hardship in their short lives. Our fosterers work so hard to give them love and socialisation - the next vital step is to find them the perfect forever home where they will be happy. Why would we work so hard to send them off with people they might not like?

If you're interested in adopting, you'll need to make an adoption appointment.

Check our Facebook page for up-to-date information about our kitties and adoption appointments!
